Who we are

Our story

Hi, we are Mr. Ink a small band from Hamburg. We consist of four members Toni (voice, guitar), Joseph (guitar), Sven (bass) and Jojo (drums and keyboard). We have been making music together since our school days. Our sound is ever changing, we like to mix different genres and get influenced by our surroundings.
