Hello, we are "Mr. Ink" (one day we talked about ink fish). We are a young band based in Hamburg, Germany. Get to know us a bit more below.

Toni is the singer and songwriter of our band. She likes to listen to bands such as The Cure, Beginners and Paolo Nutini. Her song writing process is influenced by the emotions surrounding her. Toni started making music at a young age due to her unique upbringing.

Josef is the guitarist, songwriter and cook of our band. He taught himself how to play guitar in school. Ever since not a day goes by where he is not playing. If he doesn't work on melodies, you can find him in the kitchen as a trainee.

Sven is the saxophonist and bass player in the band. His music style is influenced by Jazz and Indie bands. If he is not jamming with the band, you can find him in front of a computer. Sven is currently enrolled in Computer Science and is interested in how coding and music can be blend together.

Jojo plays the piano and drums. He is the youngest member of the band. He helps to make sure that the different sounds are well mixed in a song. You can find him in the kitchen as well, since he also training to become a cook.
